gcdyn.gpmap.AdditiveGPMap

class gcdyn.gpmap.AdditiveGPMap(mutation_effects, nonsense_phenotype=nan)[source]

Bases: GPMap

A GP map with additive effects over the amino acids in a given DNA sequence.

Parameters:

  • mutation_effects (DataFrame) – A pandas.DataFrame of phenotypic effects of amino acid states at all sites, indexed by (0-based) sites in the translated sequence and columns corresponding to amino acid identity.

  • nonsense_phenotype (float) – The phenotype of a sequence with a nonsense mutation (stop codon).
